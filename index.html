JOSEPH GUARINO

AMATEUR PHOTOGRAPHER

Joseph Guarino

American photographer based from within the cornfield covered midwest.

When it comes to photography it can be difficult to limit oneself to only a specific aspect of photography. That being said, my main focus in aviation. Very little can beat pure electricty that comes from the roar of afterburning engines, the smell of jet fuel, and the energy of the crowds. I don't, however, only limit myself to things that take to the skies. My exploration of photography started with landscapes and the outdoors.
